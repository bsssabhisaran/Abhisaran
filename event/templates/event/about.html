ABOUT Abhisaran

BSSS

“The role of culture is that it’s the form through which we as a society reflect on who we are, where we’ve been, where we hope to be.”
– Wendell Pierce
Abhisaran is a three-day international cultural fest brought to you by the Bhopal School of Social Sciences with an aim to bridge the gaps between different cultures of the world. This fest will be one of a kind with unique amalgamations of events blended together, which will give you a platform to showcase your skills and put your best foot forward. This student organized Cultural fest will be held in the second week of April 2021. It will comprise of events like Panel discussions, Interactive Sessions, Networking, and Much More
